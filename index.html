About osu! trainer (web)

A modern, browser-based reimagining of the classic osu-trainer by FunOrange. Drop an .osz file, adjust difficulty / BPM, optionally pitch-shift the audio, and download a new .osz ready to drag into osu!.

  • Audio processing powered by ffmpeg.wasm
  • Beatmap parsing & writing in pure JavaScript
  • Everything runs locally — no uploads, no servers

Tip: search "osutrainer" inside osu! to find all maps generated by this tool.
